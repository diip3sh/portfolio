# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, design clients, and engineering clients evaluating a multidisciplinary
designer, frontend developer, and creative engineer. Visitors need to quickly
understand the quality of the work, the range of disciplines covered, and whether
the creator is a strong fit for a role or project.

## Product Purpose

Present selected work and capabilities through a portfolio that demonstrates
design judgment and frontend craft directly. Success means visitors can assess
the work with confidence, remember the portfolio, and find a clear path to make
contact.

## Brand Personality

Precise, inventive, technically credible.

## Anti-references

Avoid the increasingly common developer-portfolio style represented by
chanhdai.com and its many minor variations. Do not rely on familiar developer
dashboard chrome, generic card grids, copied layout formulas, or visual decisions
that make the portfolio interchangeable with other engineering portfolios.

## Design Principles

- Make the interface itself evidence of design and engineering skill.
- Give projects enough visual space to communicate their distinct character.
- Use precision and restraint without becoming generic or anonymous.
- Create memorable composition through deliberate structure, not decoration.
- Keep paths to project details and contact clear for every audience.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus states, sufficient
color contrast, semantic structure, and reduced-motion preferences. Responsive
layouts must remain readable and usable across desktop, tablet, and phone.
